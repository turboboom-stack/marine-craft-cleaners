## Google My Business Profile

### Business Description

Marine Craft Cleaners is a mobile boat cleaning and detailing service based in Vero Beach, serving the Treasure Coast. Owner Jordan grew up on boats and spent years at Grand Bay Marine doing cleaning, winterization, and repairs. This isn't just a side hustle. It's a craft.

We come to your marina or dock. No hauling. No hassle. Just a spotless boat waiting for you.

Services include exterior wash, full interior detail, upholstery conditioning, mold treatment, oxidation removal, and ceramic sealant protection lasting 6-8 months.

Book online for instant confirmation. Serving Vero Beach, Fort Pierce, Stuart, Port St. Lucie, and Jupiter.

A clean boat means confidence on the water. Let's get yours there.
