Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026

Overview

Marine Craft Cleaners ("we," "us," or "our") operates marinecraftcleaners.com. This page describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website or request our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when booking a service or contacting us:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Boat details (length, type, location)
  • Preferred service date and package
  • Any notes or messages you include

We also collect non-personal information automatically through cookies and analytics tools, including pages visited, time on site, browser type, and general location (city/region).

How We Use Your Information

  • To contact you about your booking request and confirm appointments
  • To send service reminders and follow-up messages
  • To request reviews after a completed service
  • To improve our website and services
  • To measure the effectiveness of our advertising

We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

Google Advertising and Analytics

We use Google Ads and Google Analytics to understand how visitors find and use our site. These services may use cookies to collect information about your visits, including pages viewed and actions taken. Google may use this data to show you relevant ads on other websites.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings, or opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to support analytics and advertising functions described above. By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though some site features may not function as intended.

Third-Party Services

We use Formspree to process booking form submissions. Your form data is transmitted to and stored by Formspree in accordance with their privacy policy. We do not store form submissions on our own servers.

Data Retention

We retain your contact and booking information only as long as needed to provide services and communicate with you. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
